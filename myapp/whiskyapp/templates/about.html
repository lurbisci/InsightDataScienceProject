About the project

Insight Data Science Project

Hi my name is Laura, and I am an Insight Data Science Fellow (cohort January 2019) in San Francisco, CA. For my data science project, I used Reddit data in addition to other whisky data sets to develop an app that suggest scotch based upon the profiles of other similar scotches so that whisky lovers and converts can discover new scotches to try. This project applies collaborative filtering with neural nets in Python to make recommendations.
